# Neural_Style_Transfer_Implementation
I have implemented neural style transfer along the lines of the famous 2015 research paper on A Neural Algorithm Of Artistic Style.

Here JU.jpg is the content image (it is the main building of my college), starrynights.jpg is the landscape art of world famous artist Vincent Van Gogh used as style image and generated(1).jpg is the image that has the content of the content image and style of the style image.
